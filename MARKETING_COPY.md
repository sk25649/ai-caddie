### Copy

## Section 2: The Solution

### Headline
**Meet your caddie. The one that never sleeps.**

### Copy
A real caddie knows:
- Your miss (draws right? Slices left? Goes thin?)
- Your bag (which club leaves you 150 yards, not 145?)
- Your game (you're chasing 89, not 80)
- The hole (the bunker that looks small but eats balls)

AI Caddie does all of that. Before every shot, you see:

- **Specific aim point** — Not "hit center fairway," but "left edge of the bunker at 210"
- **Your club** — The one from your bag that works for this hole
- **The smart miss** — What to accept if things go wrong
- **What NOT to do** — The danger zone where your miss is catastrophic

No generic tips. No vague advice. Just the exact decision you need, right now, for your game.

## Section 8: FAQ

### Q: How is this different from a golf app I already use?
**A:** Other golf apps give you yardages or generic tips. AI Caddie gives you a strategy tailored to your game—your clubs, your miss, your goals. It's the difference between a GPS device and a caddie who knows you.

### Q: Will this fix my swing?
**A:** No. Your swing is yours. We help you make smarter decisions with whatever swing you have right now. Better decisions = fewer strokes, faster.

### Q: Can I use this in a tournament?
**A:** Absolutely. We have a competition mode that follows USGA Rule 4.3—strategy and club advice is hidden on-course, but your playbook and printed yardage book are perfect tournament prep.

### Q: Does it work on any course?
**A:** Yes. We have 20+ major LA courses pre-loaded. For any other course, just describe it (or we'll add it). Our caddie can work with any course description.

### Q: What if I play different courses every week?
**A:** No problem. Generate a new personalized playbook for each course. Takes 2 minutes.

### Q: How much will I improve?
**A:** Most players see 3-6 strokes improvement within their first month. But everyone's different. The key is consistency—same smart decisions, round after round.
