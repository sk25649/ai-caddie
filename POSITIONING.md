# AI Caddie — Positioning & Marketing Copy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-21

## Brand North Star

**AI Caddie is the break-90 decision coach.**

Every stroke lost is a decision mistake, not a swing mistake. We help golfers see what the pros see before every shot — then make the smarter call.

---

## Core Message

**3 smarter decisions per round = 6 fewer strokes by next season.**

For golfers chasing the break-90 milestone, every stroke counts. AI Caddie gives you a caddie's insight on every hole — what to aim at, what club to trust, and what NOT to do.

---

## Why We Exist

Breaking 90 isn't about hitting the ball farther. It's about making the right decision on every hole:
- Which club actually leaves you the best approach shot?
- Where is the real danger — where will your miss cost you most?
- What's the smart miss?

You know your swing. But on the course, decisions happen faster than you think.

**AI Caddie is your decision partner.** Pre-round playbook. Hole-by-hole strategy. Real-time caddie wisdom. Built for the break-90 push.

---

## What We Don't Do

❌ We don't teach you to swing differently.  
❌ We don't promise miracle scores.  
❌ We don't make you dependent on tech mid-round.  
❌ We're not another generic golf AI.

---

## What We Do

✅ **Personalize to your game** — Your handicap, your miss, your bag, your goals. Every recommendation is custom to you.  
✅ **Show you what the pros see** — The specific aim point, the danger zones, the smart miss for your shot shape.  
✅ **Make smarter decisions** — Better club selection, better risk management, fewer big numbers.  
✅ **Build confidence** — Walk the course knowing exactly what to do.  
✅ **Earn repeats** — Post-round review shows what worked and what to focus on next time.

---

## Target Player

**Primary:** Men and women, ages 25-55, shooting 85-100 regularly.  
- Committed enough to practice and track their game.
- Frustrated by plateau — hitting the ball well but still not breaking 90.
- Looking for an edge without overhauling their swing.

**Secondary:** Junior golfers (FCG, SCPGA, AJGA) prepping for competitive rounds.  
- Need a printed yardage book.
- Want personalized strategy for unfamiliar courses.
- Can't use live coaching mid-round — they need pre-prep.

---

## The Promise

**You have the swing. You need the decisions.**

AI Caddie is the smartest caddie in your pocket:
- Before the round: personalized playbook built for your game
- On the course: hole-by-hole strategy, specific aim points, decision guardrails
- After the round: what worked, what to repeat, what to focus on

Breaking 90 isn't magic. It's better decisions, round after round.

## Tone & Voice

**Direct, confident, no BS.**
- We talk like a caddie: honest, specific, action-oriented.
- We don't oversell. We deliver facts: "3 smarter decisions per round."
- We respect the golfer's intelligence — they know their swing, they trust us to help them think smarter.
- We avoid corporate golf-speak: no "unlock your potential," no "golf mindset," no "the power within."
